## 0. The one-paragraph bottom line<a name="0-the-one-paragraph-bottom-line"></a>

Stage-2 tested every reversible alpha lever the orientation named, plus several it did not, against
the **passive-beta null `beta_null`** (200d-SMA-gated inverse-vol top-10 majors, ~0.31–0.38 OOS
Sharpe). **One survived: a cross-sectional momentum tilt (`momentum_tilt`, +0.200 mean delta-vs-null,
positive in all three active OOS windows), confirmed robust on four independent axes.** Everything else — per-asset
trend-following, the entire derivatives-positioning channel (basis / OI / long-short / a multi-factor
ML model), keyless on-chain valuation, the BTC→alt intraday lead-lag, and every regime-overlay
refinement — is refuted, tapped, or noise. So the reversible alpha-search space is **exhausted**, with
exactly one positive candidate. That candidate is **NOT yet confirmed**: its decisive test — the
one-shot, un-peeked **reserved-holdout look** — is a human-ratified step the loop deliberately did not
take. **The single most valuable next action is the human spending that holdout on `momentum_tilt`.**
Until then, the deployable defensive baseline remains `beta_null` / `regime_voltarget` (~0.31).

## 1. The yardstick<a name="1-the-yardstick"></a>

`beta_null` is the Stage-0 passive-beta null: top-10-liquidity (PIT, monthly) majors, a binary BTC
200d-SMA risk-on/off gate, inverse-vol weighting, `vol_target=0.50`, realistic `vip2_bnb` costs. Its
defensive virtue is **clean full-cash-in-bear** (0.00 in 2022). Every Stage-2 idea was A/B'd against it
on the OOS walk-forward harness (`zcrypto stress`), read on the **cost-adjusted paired delta-vs-null
with a stationary-bootstrap CI**, never gross ending value. `regime_voltarget` (the same gate with the
vol-target lever, ~0.31 OOS) is the established deployable; `beta_null` is the comparison null.

## 3. The one edge — `momentum_tilt`<a name="3-the-one-edge-%E2%80%94-momentum_tilt"></a>

A cross-sectional momentum tilt on `beta_null`: tilt the inverse-vol weights toward coins with the
strongest recent relative return (`w_i *= exp(+k·z_i)` over a trailing-return z-score, k=1.0, 30d),
renormalized, no look-ahead. **Result: +0.200 mean delta-vs-`beta_null`, positive in all three active
OOS windows** — the first overlay in the whole project to beat the passive null. Confirmed on **four
independent axes:**

- **Direction** (iter-47): +0.200, positive every window.
- **Lookback** (iter-48): +0.14 to +0.21 across 14/30/60/90d — not a 30d-overfit knob.
- **Cost** (iter-49): +0.194 at 2× the turnover-scaling maker-fill haircut — not a cost artifact.
- **Strength** (iter-50): clean monotonic-then-plateau dose-response (k0.5 +0.107 → k1.0 +0.200 → k1.5
  +0.221 → k2.0 +0.223) — the signature of a real signal; k=1.0 is the robust all-windows-positive default.

**Honest caveats (do not over-read):** per-window daily-delta CIs mostly straddle 0; the deflated Sharpe
is uncomputable from a single-trial register (which is *why* the holdout matters); the magnitude leans
on 2024 (though positive in all windows); it is **1 positive of ~8 overlays tried** — a real
false-discovery risk that only the un-peeked holdout defeats. Tracked in **`T0024`**.

## 4. The deployable today<a name="4-the-deployable-today"></a>

Until `momentum_tilt` is confirmed, the deployable defensive baseline is **`beta_null` /
`regime_voltarget` (~0.31 OOS Sharpe)** — robust, cheap, beta-timing not alpha. Every Stage-2 overlay
either failed to beat it or (momentum) is unconfirmed. This is consistent with the orientation's
disciplined prior: beyond a regime-gated passive-beta rule, capturable edge on this data is scarce.

## 5. Parked decisions (human-ratified — the loop did not take these)<a name="5-parked-decisions-human-ratified-%E2%80%94-the-loop-did-not-take-these"></a>

1. **Spend the reserved-holdout look on `momentum_tilt`** (`T0024`) — the one-shot, un-peeked,
   multiple-testing-proof confirmation. **The single highest-value next action.** Pre-register the exact
   recipe (30d, k=1.0) before peeking. If it survives → the project has its first deployable cross-sectional
   edge; if not → the +0.200 was in-sample overfitting and the passive-beta ceiling stands.
1. **Credentialed on-chain data** (`T0021`) — the keyless NVM proxy failed, but the *better* metric
   (realized-cap-based MVRV-Z/NUPL) is credentialed-only (Coin Metrics PRO / CryptoQuant / BigQuery). The
   keyless failure does **not** condemn MVRV-Z; the head-to-head vs the SMA gate is parked on that data.
1. **Shelve derivatives-positioning** (`T0023`) — well-evidenced (6 single-factor signals + ML, none beats
   the null), but a strategic-channel-kill is left for an attended decision.

## 6. Honest meta-conclusion + the next frontier<a name="6-honest-meta-conclusion--the-next-frontier"></a>

After Phase-1 (OHLCV-ML, exhausted — `T0018`) and Phase-2 Stage-2 (~20 reversible iterations), the search
has produced **exactly one edge candidate (momentum), unconfirmed**, and otherwise confirmed that the
OHLCV / regime / derivatives / keyless-on-chain / intraday-lead-lag space is tapped. The reusable
apparatus built along the way is substantial (the derivatives + on-chain fetchers, the cross-sectional
tilt machinery, the offline lead-lag probe, the regime debounce). **The next frontier is genuinely-new
information or a decisive holdout:**

- **First:** the human spends the `momentum_tilt` holdout (the cheap decisive step that's already teed up).
- **If momentum fails / for the next phase:** the only untried levers need new data — **credentialed
  on-chain valuation** (`T0021`, parked) or **order-book / microstructure** (`T0010` remainder) — both
  heavy and out of the unattended loop's reversible scope. The daily-OHLCV / keyless space is closed.

The disciplined reading: the project has a robust ~0.31 defensive deployable and one promising-but-unconfirmed
alpha; whether it has *more* than beta-timing now hinges on a single human-ratified holdout look.
